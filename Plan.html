Plan

Once we have identified the type of pigment we want to produce, we plan out the process for making it. This involve identifying the specific fungal strain that produces the desired pigment, as well as determining the optimal growth conditions for that strain. We also need to plan out the materials and equipment need for the production process.

Selection of Fungal Strain for

Pigments Production

Once we have identified the specific type of fungal pigment to be produced, the next step is to we plan out the production process. This involves several key considerations, such as the selection of a fungal strain that produces the desired pigment and the determination of the optimal growth conditions for that strain.

Optimization of Growth Conditions for Pigment Production

Pigment Production

To select a suitable fungal strain, it is important to consider factors such as pigment yield, growth rate, and the ability of the fungus to thrive under specific growth conditions. For example, some fungal strains may produce high yields of pigment but may require specific nutrients or environmental conditions to do so. Once a suitable fungal strain has been identified, Our next step is to optimize the growth conditions to maximize pigment production.

Extraction and Purification of

Pigment

The growth conditions for fungal pigment production can vary depending on the specific strain being used, but some common factors to consider include temperature, humidity, light, and the composition of the growth medium. We test different growth conditions to determine the optimal conditions for pigment production. Additionally, our production process involve specific steps such as extraction and purification of the pigment, which is considered by us in the planning phase.

Overall, the planning phase is critical for ensuring the successful production of fungal pigments. By carefully selecting a suitable fungal strain and optimizing the growth conditions for that strain, it is possible for us to maximize pigment yield and quality, and create a more sustainable alternative to synthetic colorants.
